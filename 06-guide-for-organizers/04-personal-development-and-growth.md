# Personal Development and Growth for Organizers

Being a BitDevs organizer is an opportunity for both professional and personal growth. You gain skills in leadership, communication, technical literacy, and community management, all while contributing to a global movement. 

## Ways to Grow as an Organizer

- **Deepen Technical Knowledge:** Join open-source discussions, attend Bitcoin developer calls, participate in [Btrust Builders](https://pathways.btrust.tech/) pathways, and explore relevant codebases.
- **Develop Soft Skills:** Practice facilitation, conflict resolution, and constructive feedback exchange.
- **Build Visibility and Credibility:** Share event recaps, lessons learned, and insights on Nostr, X (formerly Twitter), or other platforms.
- **Network Widely:** Engage with organizers from other cities, conferences, and meetups to exchange ideas and experiences.
- **Seek Mentorship:** Reach out to experienced facilitators or sponsor representatives for guidance and feedback.

## Additional Opportunities for Btrust-Supported Organizers

- Participate in capacity-building sessions and workshops.
- Eligibility for cross-location collaborations and speaker exchanges.
- Access to travel or training grants, when available.
